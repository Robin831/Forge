category: Fixed
- **Failed pipeline now clears bead assignee on release** - When a pipeline fails and the bead is released back to open status, the assignee is now also cleared so the poller can re-dispatch the bead. Previously, the assignee set during claim was never cleared on failure, causing the bead to remain permanently invisible to the poller. Both `releaseBead` (pipeline.go) and `resetBead` (shutdown.go) now include `--assignee=` in the `bd update` call. The `forge queue retry` handler also clears the assignee so manually retried beads are immediately visible on the next poll cycle. (Forge-3kdt)
